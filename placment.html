What Does Permanent Hiring Involve?

Permanent hiring in IT involves recruiting individuals for long-term roles in technology-focused firms. They drive software development, manage IT infrastructure, and innovate digitally. With benefits like health care and retirement plans, these hires are essential for building skilled teams to support tech advancements and business growth.

Explore Our Direct Placement Offerings

At Leewayzon consultancy, we understand the importance of finding the right talent to drive your organisation's success. Our Direct Placement services offer a streamlined approach to connecting employers with top-tier candidates for permanent positions, ensuring a perfect match for both parties.

Our Direct Placement Procedure

  • Needs Assessment: We start by conducting a thorough assessment of your hiring needs, including understanding the job requirements, desired qualifications, and company culture.
  • Candidate Sourcing:Leveraging our extensive network and targeted recruitment strategies, we identify and attract qualified candidates who match your criteria.
  • Screening and Selection: We carefully screen candidates through interviews, assessments, and reference checks to ensure they meet your standards for expertise and fit.
  • Interview Coordination: We coordinate and schedule interviews between you and the shortlisted candidates, providing support and guidance throughout the process.
  • Offer Negotiation: Once you've selected the ideal candidate, we assist with offer negotiations, ensuring a smooth transition from candidate to employee.
